The Haberman Research Group is housed in the Walker Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin. The group studies a wide range of topics in theoretical and applied acoustics, with a focus on acoustic metamaterials, nondestructive evaluation... Applications of the group's work are broad (ocean acoustics, architectural acoustics, automotive industry, etc...)
